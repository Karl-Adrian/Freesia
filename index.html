About Us

Kenyan-Owned. Professionally Driven.

Founded in 2019, Freesia Cleaning Services Ltd is a registered Kenyan company with a proven track record in commercial, residential, and industrial cleaning. Our main office is in Nairobi, with satellite offices across the country for real-time service delivery.

We understand that hygiene is not optional — it is essential. Our dedicated teams are equipped with modern tools and eco-friendly products to uphold the highest standards every time.
